marvel.txt:
This dataset contains the data of partnerships between characters in the marvel universe, nodes are characters (heros or villians) and the edges are whom they are partners with.

marvel.csv:
This is the cleaned verison of the marvel.txt data

results.csv:
This dataset contains data from the NCAA 2017 tournament, nodes are are individual teams and edges connect to other teams if they versed each other in a game. One attribute of the edges is
whether a sink team won/lost a game and the scores of the game.
